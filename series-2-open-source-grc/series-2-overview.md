# Series 2 Overview

> Series 1 showed how to operate Integrated Risk Management on ServiceNow. Series 2 steps back and presents the underlying framework as an original point of view.

Series 2 is a **thought-leadership framework** for modern risk management. Rather than a build tutorial, it lays out a coherent way of thinking about enterprise risk — from the full map of risk down to how mature an organization's capability is.

## The framework, in sequence

Read the sections in order; each layer builds on the previous one:

1. **[The Risk Universe](../01-risk-universe/README.md)** — define the whole problem space.
2. **[The 9 Domains](../02-nine-domains/README.md)** — segment it into ownable areas.
3. **[The 4 Lines of Defense](../03-four-lines-of-defense/README.md)** — assign ownership, oversight, and assurance.
4. **[Intelligence (AI)](../04-intelligence-ai/README.md)** — augment every domain and line with automation and analytics.
5. **[The 5-Level Maturity Model](../05-maturity-model/README.md)** — measure where you stand and chart the path forward.
6. **[Synthesis](../06-synthesis/README.md)** — how it all fits together into one integrated view.

## Why this framing

Most risk programs are fragmented because they start with tools. This framework starts with a complete map of risk, structures it, assigns accountability, applies intelligence, and measures maturity — an integrated practice rather than a pile of disconnected controls.
