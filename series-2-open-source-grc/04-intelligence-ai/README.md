# Intelligence (AI)

> The framework's differentiator. Structure tells you where risk lives; intelligence tells you what is happening and what to do next.

The **Intelligence (AI) layer** is what turns a static risk framework into a living one. It applies automation, analytics, and AI across the Risk Universe, the 9 Domains, and the 4 Lines of Defense — continuously sensing, scoring, and recommending rather than relying on periodic manual review.

## What intelligence adds

- **Continuous sensing** — signals from across the environment instead of point-in-time snapshots
- **Automated scoring** — risks and controls assessed dynamically as conditions change
- **Decision support** — recommendations and prioritization for each line of defense
- **Assurance acceleration** — evidence gathered and tested automatically

## How intelligence maps to the framework

- **First Line** — automated, self-monitoring controls
- **Second Line** — anomaly detection and continuous policy monitoring
- **Third Line** — AI-assisted audit and evidence sampling
- **Across the 9 Domains** — domain-specific models and analytics

## Why this is the differentiator

Most GRC programs bolt on reporting. This framework treats intelligence as a first-class layer, which is what enables an organization to climb the **5-Level Maturity Model** from reactive to predictive.
