## Overview

Membership in the TechTalk with Bill IRM community gives you structured access to content, artifacts, and peer learning beyond the free handbook. Three tiers address different stages of your IRM journey: CSA Student (entry-level platform learners), Architect Pro (practitioners building or operating IRM programs), and Enterprise Insider (teams and organizations deploying IRM at scale). Free access to this handbook's core content has no tier requirement — membership unlocks the deeper, implementation-accelerating layer.

## Key concepts

* **Tiered depth, not paywalled basics** — The handbook's explanatory content is free for everyone. Membership unlocks the implementation-accelerating artifacts (editable source files, the full update set library, advanced templates) and the community learning layer (Q&A, office hours, enterprise patterns).
* **Community learning** — Membership includes access to a community Q&A space where practitioners share implementation questions, solutions, and lessons learned. This is where you get answers to the "I followed the video but my instance behaves differently" problems that documentation cannot fully anticipate.
* **Right tier for your role** — CSA Student is right if you are studying for your first ServiceNow certification or are new to IRM. Architect Pro is right if you are implementing IRM for an organization. Enterprise Insider is right if you are leading a multi-team or multi-domain IRM deployment.
