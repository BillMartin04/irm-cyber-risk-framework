## Overview

Architect Pro is the practitioner tier — designed for GRC consultants, ServiceNow IRM architects, and in-house risk and compliance professionals who are actively designing or operating an IRM program. It provides the full artifact library, the CIS-RC certification prep path, and direct access to deeper technical sessions and office hours. If CSA Student is the learning tier, Architect Pro is the building tier.

## Key concepts

* **Who it's for** — ServiceNow IRM architects and consultants implementing the platform for clients or employers; GRC program managers who own the ServiceNow IRM configuration; risk and compliance professionals with ServiceNow admin or configurator access; anyone preparing for the CIS-RC exam.
* **Depth of content** — Architect Pro content goes beyond what is covered in the public videos — it includes implementation patterns, design decisions and their trade-offs, configuration edge cases discovered in real engagements, and worked examples from banking, healthcare, and government deployments (anonymized).
* **Office hours** — Monthly live sessions where Bill works through implementation questions, reviews submitted architecture questions, and demonstrates techniques not covered in published videos. Sessions are recorded and available to tier members after the live session.

## What's Included

* **Full update set library** — All update sets across all modules: foundations, assessments, KRI indicators, third-party risk, CRI Profile, CAM/POAM workflow, and any new sets published during your membership. Each set includes a README with prerequisites, compatibility notes, and implementation guidance.
* **Full template library** — Every template from the Templates and Downloads page, including the executive Assessment Report (PPTX), Third-Party Risk Assessment Questionnaire (both Tier 1 and Tier 2 versions), POAM Tracker, Risk Appetite Statement, and Vendor Risk Tiering Criteria.
* **CIS-RC certification prep bundle** — Full study guide, exam blueprint mapping, 80-question practice exam (two sets of 40 questions covering different domain areas), and a 4-week study plan with daily tasks.
* **Architect Pro deep-dives** — Written walkthroughs (with screenshots) of advanced configuration topics not covered in public videos: scoped app architecture for multi-domain deployments, ACL design for three-lines-of-defense role separation, IntegrationHub spoke configuration for SIEM and vulnerability scanner feeds, Advanced Risk quantitative scenario configuration.
* **Monthly office hours** — Live Q&A sessions (recorded). Submit questions in advance via the member portal; questions are addressed in priority order during the session.
* **Community Q&A access** — Same community access as CSA Student, with an Architect Pro label that signals your implementation-level experience to other members.

## Practical Value for Consultants

For ServiceNow IRM consultants, the Architect Pro tier delivers concrete engagement acceleration:

* The full update set library cuts initial configuration time in a new client engagement by eliminating from-scratch builds for standard module patterns.
* The template library provides client-ready deliverables (risk registers, assessment questionnaires, executive reports) that can be adapted and delivered directly.
* The CIS-RC certification demonstrates to clients that your IRM implementation capability is vendor-validated.
* The deep-dive documentation covers the configuration edge cases that cost the most time on real engagements — cases where the standard documentation is silent.

{% hint style="info" %}
CIS-RC-certified consultants command meaningfully higher market rates for ServiceNow IRM engagements. The Architect Pro tier's CIS-RC prep bundle, combined with the hands-on update set exercises, is the most efficient preparation path available outside of formal ServiceNow training courses.
